Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the batch size at Gaining Grades?
+
We maintain small batch sizes of 10–15 students per batch. This ensures every student gets personal attention and their doubts are solved promptly.
Are demo classes available before admission?
+
Yes! We offer a free demo class for all new students. You can attend one session before deciding. Call us on 8433601642 to schedule your free demo.
What are the class timings?
+
Classes run Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM. We have multiple time slots available so students can choose as per their school schedule.
Do you provide study material?
+
Yes, we provide comprehensive study material, practice sheets, previous year question papers and weekly test papers — all included in the course fee.
How can I take admission?
+
Simply fill the enquiry form below or call us at 8433601642 / 8433589382. You can also WhatsApp us and our team will guide you through the admission process.
Is there any fee concession available?
+
We offer special discounts for siblings, early batch registration, and meritorious students. Please contact us directly to know about current offers and concessions.
